Save Lebanese Hosipitality

HTML, CSS, SCSS, JS, PHP, MySQL

Developed and designed a mobile responsive landing page for the syndicate of restaurants in Lebanon. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, cPanel, MySQL.

April 2020 - V.1.0 Online petition form for government aid following the covid-19 crisis. Visitors could sign the petition, upload their CV and contact the syndicate. This was my first full attempt at development.

August 2020 - V.2.0 Pivot to an online donation information page linking to a the GoFundMe account following the explosion at the port of Beirut. This involved a complete revamp of the look and feel and Introduced google analytics.
